Describe The Game.

Ping pong, is a sport where two players hit a lightweight ball back and forth across a table using small paddles, The game takes place on a rectangular table divided by a line, Players score points when their opponent fails to return the ball within the rules, Play is fast and requires quick reflexes. The game is typically played to `11` points, and matches are usually best of `5` games.
